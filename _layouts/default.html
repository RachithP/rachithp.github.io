Rachith Prakash

I'm a research engineer at Intel. My interests and experiences are shaped around building software solutions across the intersection of Computer Vision, Robotics and Embodied-AI domains. I primarily develop software frameworks, and functionalities to support research in Embodied AI, Computer Vision domains. I'm currently working on SPEAR - A Simulator for Photorealistic Embodied AI Research .

I obtained my Masters in Robotics from the University of Maryland, College Park in 2020. During my Masters, I explored Bayesian Deep Learning with primary focus on Reinforcement Learning. I obtained my Bachelors in Electronics and Communications Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Karntaka Surathkal (NITK) in 2016. At NITK, I worked on developing Brain Computer Interface Applications as my final project and was advised by Dr. Deepu Vijayasenan.
